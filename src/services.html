FULL STACK DEVELOPMENT: LET'S BEGIN

Full stack development is concerned with both frontend(user side of website) and backend(server side of website). There are 6 stages involved in web development: Planning,design,development,testing ,launching and maintainence. Frontend development is concerned with the elements an user sees and backend development is concerned with managing api requests, data storage and retrieval from databases.
